How BridgeCare Works

Our platform brings together different stakeholders to create a seamless support system for those in need

Healthcare Centers

Provide medical assistance and maintain comprehensive health records for homeless individuals.

Food Providers

Donate meals and track food distribution to ensure no one goes hungry.

NGOs

Coordinate resources and monitor the assistance provided to individuals in need.

Volunteers

Register homeless individuals and track their progress through our support system.

Donors

Contribute resources and track how your donations are making a tangible difference.

Admin Dashboard

Monitor all activities and coordinate between different stakeholders efficiently.
